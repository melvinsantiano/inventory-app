📖 How to Use

📝 Adding Products

  • Fill in Item Name, Price, and Initial Stock
  • Click Add Product to save
  • Adding existing product increases its stock

🔄 Managing Stock

  • Click + to restock
  • Click - to record a sale
  • ⚠️ Items with stock below 5 will flash red (low stock warning)

🔍 Finding Products

  • Use the search bar at the top
  • Also available in the hamburger menu ☰

🔄 Sorting

  • Sort by Price - toggles ↑↓
  • Sort by Stock - toggles ↑↓
  • Sort by Name - toggles A↔Z

📈 Trends

  • 📈 +X% = Stock increased
  • 📉 -X% = Stock decreased
  • ➖ 0% = No change

🗑️ Deleting

  • Use trash icon to delete individual items
  • Click Reset Inventory to clear everything

📊 Daily Sales (New!)

  • Click ☰ → Daily Sales to view today's stats
  • See total sales, revenue, restocks, and profit
  • Transaction history with timestamps
  • Data resets daily at midnight
